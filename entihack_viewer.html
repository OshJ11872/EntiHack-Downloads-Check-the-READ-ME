You got them right?

We use something called a honeypot, which attracts hackers — it looks like a real, weakly-protected login page or server, but it's fake and only exists to get probed. Once someone attacks it, we use the firewall to block them entirely: after a few suspicious attempts, their connection is refused outright, so they can't reach anything real.
